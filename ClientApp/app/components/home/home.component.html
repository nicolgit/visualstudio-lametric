HOW TO

  • Authenticate on visual studio online (VSO) using the button on this page
  • Write the full URL of the VSO project you want to monitors checkins (TFS projects only, NO GIT at the moment)
  • Click the TEST button to verify if everything is working fine
  • Click the SAVE button
  • On your Lametric smart clock(r) look for "Visual Studio Online Checkins" app
  • Write the email used here to log in here in email field on Lametric!
  • Enjoy your checkins:-)
